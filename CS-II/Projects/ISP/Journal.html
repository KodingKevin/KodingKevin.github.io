Role Research

Development Engineer - This role is responsible with the leading design and the product of this group. I personally think I'm suitable for this role considering I often like to start with an idea and improving as we go.

QA Engineer - This role is responsible for the quality of the product and in this case is to make sure that there isn't any error within the project. In other words the QA Engineer are the debuggers of the team. I think I'm suitable for this role as well however decoding and debugging has never been my strong suit.

Project Manager - This role is responsible to make plans and that all task are delivered properly within the group. This role requires someone to have skills such as leadership and team management. However these traits I don't obtain which mean I would not be suitable for this role.

Product Manager - This role is responsible for the overall development of product and is like the brain of the team that creates the different ideas. This role also comes great responsibility along with the project manager and as someone who stress a lot I would say this role isn't suitable for me.

Release Engineer - This role is responsible to make proper judgement on whether or not the product is ready to be released. This role requires someone to have some overall experience so they can make the proper call on the project. As someone who's basically an amature in this field and have basically no experience I would not be that suitable for this role.
